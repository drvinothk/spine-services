Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Spine Services ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to spineservices.org. This page explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

This website does not collect any personal data directly through forms or login systems. The only interaction that involves personal data is when you book a meeting through the Calendly scheduling widget embedded on this site.

2. Calendly

When you use the Calendly booking widget to schedule a meeting, Calendly collects your name, email address, and any other information you provide. This data is processed by Calendly in accordance with their own Privacy Policy, available at calendly.com/privacy. We receive meeting confirmation details to manage appointments.

3. Cookies

Calendly sets cookies when the booking widget loads on this page. These cookies enable the scheduling widget to function properly. By accepting the cookie notice on this site, you consent to these cookies being set.

4. Email Communications

If you contact us at connect@spineservices.org, we will use your email address and the information you share solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

5. Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (Microsoft) and uses Google Search Console for webmaster verification. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle data.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Data collected through Calendly is retained according to Calendly's data retention policies. You can request deletion of your Calendly data by contacting Calendly directly.

7. Your Rights

If you are based in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction with data protection laws, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. For any privacy-related requests, please email us at connect@spineservices.org.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions, please contact: connect@spineservices.org
